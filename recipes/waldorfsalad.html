Waldorf Salad

Description

This Waldorf salad recipe is delicious, and you can vary the ingredients to your preference. Try adding diced, roasted chicken to make this salad a meal!

Ingredients (Servings: 1)

  • ½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • ⅛ teaspoon salt
  • 3 apples -- peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 cup thinly sliced celery
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts
  • ½ cup raisins (Optional)

Steps

  1. Whisk together mayonnaise, sugar, lemon juice, and salt in a serving bowl.
  2. Stir in apples, celery, walnuts, and raisins.
  3. Cover and chill in the refrigerator until ready to serve.
